Project cards display text and metrics only — no dashboard screenshots or preview images. Render the four cards as a **horizontal scroll carousel**. Cards sit side by side; the user scrolls horizontally to browse them. The carousel must support both desktop and mobile.

**Carousel Auto-Scroll Behavior:**

- Auto-scroll through the cards in a continuous loop. After the fourth card (Investment Intelligence), seamlessly loop back to the first card (Revenue Command).
- When the user drags or swipes a card manually, auto-scroll pauses.
- Auto-scroll resumes automatically after 5 seconds of inactivity (no dragging).
- User can grab and drag any card manually at any time to override auto-scroll.

**Mobile Carousel Behavior:** On mobile, display one card at a time with approximately 10-20% of the next card visible at the edge to signal scrollability.
